<sup>*</sup> For the singular ACC/LOC, the _i_ is removed from the stem for -is/ys nouns. E.g. _brolį_, _arkylį_ in the ACC or _brolyje_,_arklyje_ in the LOC. This is because iį and iy are not valid dipthongs.
